Darkmira Tour PHP 2017

The unmissable event about
Security and software factory in PHP ecosystems

2 days of learning, discoveries and interactions

Come and join us

May Saturday 27 & Sunday 28, 2017 at the Universidade Catlica de Braslia

Discoveries

Expect to discover in these 2 days thelatest news of the PHP ecosystem: best practices, tools, methodologies and others. There are over 20 talks from international and national speakers sharing their knowledge.

Interaction

Interact along with 400 attendees on several coffee breaks, demonstrations and networking held in the business and community spaces. Share your knowledge in the unconference area: everyone can submit a lightning talk.

Accessible

We are located in a central point in Brazil as a choice. To be accessible through any airport in the country is our goal, specially to bring together all of the BrazilianPHP communities.Flight quotes are available by filling out our document.

Our international Keynote

  • Damien Seguy CTO at Exakat Ltd. / Elephpant breeder

Damien is CTO at Exakat Ltd., a company specialized in PHP code quality solutions for the industry. He leads the development of the exakat static analysis engine, that automatically audit code for compatibility, security and sustainable code. He has worked on three continents with web applications, documentation author, and elephpant breeder. He has been credited for sharing enthusiasm and know-how about code generation, gremlin language, and camembert.

Where and when will the event be located?

The event will take place on May 27 & 28, 2017, at the prestigious Universidade Catlica de Brasilia. For the conference happen in a relaxed and professional atmosphere, the University will make their campus and infrastructure available: 2 auditoriums (1 of 700 and 1 of 200) and an area for coffee breaks / sponsors plus a space for PHP communities.
